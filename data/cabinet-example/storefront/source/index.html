The Story

Born from Hogwarts.
Built for Mischief.

What started as enchanted sweets smuggled through dormitory corridors became the most beloved joke shop in the wizarding world. Fred and George Weasley turned their talent for troublemaking into an art form, leaving Hogwarts in a blaze of fireworks and founding Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes at 93 Diagon Alley.

Every product in our collection is hand-crafted, rigorously tested (often on unsuspecting siblings), and guaranteed to deliver wonder, laughter, or a spectacular transformation. We believe magic should be joyful, surprising, and just a little bit dangerous.
