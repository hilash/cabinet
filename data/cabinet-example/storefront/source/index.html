Our Story

From Hogwarts Dorm Room to Diagon Alley

It started with a Canary Cream in a Gryffindor common room and a dream to make the wizarding world laugh. Fred and George Weasley turned 10 Galleons of seed money, a talent for trouble, and an absolute refusal to be ordinary into the most beloved shop on Diagon Alley.

Today, Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes is more than a joke shop. It's a magical empire — from experimental candy that pushes the boundaries of enchantment to operations that ship wonder across the wizarding world.

Every product in our catalog started as a mad idea scribbled on a napkin, tested in our legendary laboratory, and perfected until even the Ministry couldn't argue with the results (usually).
