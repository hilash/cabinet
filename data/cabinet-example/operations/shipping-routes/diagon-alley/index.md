# Diagon Alley

**"Number 93 — the brightest shopfront on the street, and we intend to keep it that way."**

Diagon Alley is our flagship location and the beating heart of Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes. Every Galleon of revenue started here, and it still accounts for 60% of total sales. Treat this route like royalty.

## Floo Network Details

- **Primary fireplace:** Back storeroom, unit #93-B
- **Backup fireplace:** Leaky Cauldron (arrangement with Tom, costs 2 Sickles/use)
- **Capacity:** 40 parcels per Floo cycle, 3 cycles per morning window
- **Soot mitigation:** All candy shipments double-wrapped in enchanted parchment since the Meltdrop Shimmer Incident

## Partner Shops

We supply several Diagon Alley retailers on wholesale terms:

- **Flourish & Blotts** — they carry our Spell-Check Quills near the register (impulse buy goldmine)
- **Madam Malkin's** — novelty items in the waiting area
- **Quality Quidditch Supplies** — Quidditch-themed prank bundles (Lee Jordan's idea, naturally)

## Walk-In Wholesale

Hogwarts professors and event planners can arrange bulk pickup at the shop. Minimum order: 50 units. George handles these personally because Fred tends to "throw in extras" that aren't on the invoice.
