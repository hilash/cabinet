## Product Categories

- **Luxury / Romantic** — Premium sweets for date nights, gifts, and "I'm sorry I turned you into a canary" apology boxes. Higher margin, lower volume.
- **Novelty / Student** — The bread and butter. Affordable, shareable, and designed to be the most interesting thing at the Gryffindor table.
- **Classic / Prank** — Our heritage line. The products that built the brand. Updated, upgraded, and still reliably hilarious.
- **Experimental / Chaos** — Things George hasn't signed off on yet. Proceed with caution and possibly a shield charm.

## Catalog Rules

1. Every product must have a Ministry safety rating before it hits the shelf (even if Fred thinks the rating system is "a creativity tax")
2. Pricing must align with [[Pricing Strategy]] tier structure
3. Packaging must follow [[Brand Guide]] color and voice standards
4. No product ships without at least one good joke on the label (see [[Joke Book]])
