# Fred Weasley

**Co-Founder — Product & Marketing**

**"Every great product starts with someone saying 'what if?' and everyone else saying 'please don't.' I'm the 'what if' guy."**

---

## Role

Fred is the creative engine of Weasleys' Wizard Wheezes. He generates product ideas, writes marketing copy, runs in-store demos, and is personally responsible for at least 60% of the energy in any room he enters. His title is "Co-Founder" but his actual job is "the reason people walk in the door."

## Strengths

- **Ideas.** Fred generates more product concepts in a week than most companies produce in a year. Not all of them are good (see: the Exploding Quiche incident). But enough of them are brilliant that the hit rate more than justifies the chaos.
- **Launches.** Nobody launches a product like Fred. The in-store events, the Daily Prophet teaser campaigns, the moment of reveal — he treats every launch like opening night at a theatre. And it works.
- **Showmanship.** Fred on the shop floor is our single greatest sales asset. He demos products with the energy of a carnival barker and the timing of a professional comedian. Customers buy things they didn't know existed five minutes ago.

## Working With Fred

- He responds best to "yes, and..." energy. Shoot down an idea too fast and he'll do it anyway, just without telling you.
- If you need him to slow down, get George or Verity. They're the only two people who can reliably get Fred to pause and think.
- He's surprisingly good at taking feedback on creative work — IF you frame it as making the idea better, not killing it.
- Don't schedule him for anything before 9am. He's not a morning person. He's a "burst through the door at 9:47 with three new ideas" person.

## Favourite Quote

> "The world is full of boring. Our job is to fix that, one prank at a time."
