# Brand Guidelines

How ChocoBar Co looks, sounds, and feels — everywhere.

## Voice & Tone

We sound like a friend who *really* knows chocolate. Warm, a little playful, never preachy. We're proud of our ingredients but we don't lecture.

- **Do:** "Real cocoa butter. Real cane sugar. Really, really good."
- **Don't:** "Our proprietary blend of ethically-sourced cacao delivers an unparalleled tasting experience."

> **Our mission:** Make the best-tasting candy bars on the internet, from ingredients you can actually pronounce, at a price that doesn't make you flinch.

## Writing Principles

- **Lead with flavor.** Always describe how it *tastes* before how it's *made*.
- **Keep it short.** If a sentence has a comma, see if it can be two sentences.
- **Be specific.** Not "premium chocolate" — "72% Ecuadorian dark chocolate with hints of red fruit."
- **Skip the jargon.** *Single-origin* is fine. *Terroir-driven bean-to-bar artisanal micro-batch* is not.

## Brand Lines

Approved taglines and one-liners for use across packaging, ads, social, and web:

- **Primary tagline:** "Stupid good chocolate."
- "Made simple. Tastes anything but."
- "Ingredients you know. Flavor you won't forget."
- "The candy bar grew up."
- "No weird stuff. Just chocolate."
- "Melt-in-your-mouth honest."
- "One ingredient list you'll actually want to read."
- "Snack like you give a damn."
- "Less filler. More flavor. Way more chocolate."
- "Your 3pm just got a serious upgrade."

**Usage notes:**
- The primary tagline ("Stupid good chocolate.") should appear on all packaging and hero sections.
- Rotate the others across social posts, email subject lines, and ad creatives — keep it fresh.
- Always lowercase after the first word unless it's a proper noun. We're casual, not sloppy.

## Visual Identity

**Primary colors:**
- Chocolate Brown `#3C1A0B` — backgrounds, headers
- Cream `#FFF8F0` — text on dark backgrounds
- Caramel Gold `#D4A54A` — accents, CTAs, highlights

**Typography:**
- Headlines: bold, warm, slightly rounded
- Body: clean sans-serif, generous line-height
- Packaging: custom hand-lettered feel for bar names

**Photography style:**
- Close-up texture shots (melting chocolate, crunchy bits, sea salt crystals)
- Warm lighting, shallow depth of field
- Real people eating chocolate — not stock-photo-perfect, just genuine enjoyment
