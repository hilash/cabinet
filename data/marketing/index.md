# Marketing

We sell candy bars on the internet. Our marketing is direct, visual, and built to make people crave chocolate at 2pm on a Tuesday.

## Strategy

**Channels:** Instagram (primary), TikTok (growth), Email (retention), Blog (SEO)

**Core message:** Real chocolate, real ingredients, delivered to your door. No gimmicks.

**Target audience:** Health-conscious millennials and Gen Z who read ingredient labels but still want chocolate that *actually tastes good*.
