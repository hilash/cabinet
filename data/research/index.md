# Research

Everything we build starts here. Market data, competitor analysis, flavor science, and ingredient sourcing — this is where we separate gut feelings from real insights.

## What's Inside

- <a data-wiki-link="true" data-page-name="Competitive Landscape" href="#page:competitive-landscape" class="wiki-link">Competitive Landscape</a> — Who we're up against and where we win
- <a data-wiki-link="true" data-page-name="Flavor Trends" href="#page:flavor-trends" class="wiki-link">Flavor Trends</a> — Interactive chart of what's hot in candy right now
- <a data-wiki-link="true" data-page-name="Ingredient Specs" href="#page:ingredient-specs" class="wiki-link">Ingredient Specs</a> — Sourcing standards, nutrition schemas, and allergen tracking

## Research Process

1. **Identify** — Spot a trend, competitor move, or customer signal
2. **Validate** — Pull data from sales, surveys, and social listening
3. **Document** — Write it up here so the whole team has context
4. **Act** — Feed insights into <a data-wiki-link="true" data-page-name="Product Catalog" href="#page:catalog" class="wiki-link">Product Catalog</a> and <a data-wiki-link="true" data-page-name="Marketing" href="#page:marketing" class="wiki-link">Marketing</a> decisions
