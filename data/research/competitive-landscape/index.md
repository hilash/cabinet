# Competitive Landscape

Last updated Q1 2026. We review this quarterly and after any major competitor launch.

## Market Overview

The premium candy bar market is growing at 8% annually, driven by health-conscious consumers willing to pay more for clean ingredients and ethical sourcing. Our sweet spot is the $4–7 price range where taste meets transparency.

## Key Takeaways

**Where we win:** ChocoBar Co sits at the intersection of *premium taste* and *transparent sourcing* — without the health-food austerity of Hu or the mass-market compromises of Mars. Our DTC-first model gives us margin and customer data that retail-dependent brands don't have.

**Biggest threat:** Tony's Chocolonely expanding their online subscription offering. Their brand loyalty is strong.

**Opportunity:** None of these competitors have a strong subscription + customization play. Our build-your-own box feature (see <a data-wiki-link="true" data-page-name="Product Catalog" href="#page:catalog" class="wiki-link">Product Catalog</a>) is a real differentiator.
