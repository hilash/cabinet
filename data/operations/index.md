# Operations

Everything that happens between "we need more cocoa" and "your order has shipped."

## Supply Chain

We work with 5 key suppliers across 4 countries. Open **suppliers.csv** in the sidebar to view and edit the full supplier directory — you can double-click any cell to update it.

Lead times range from 7 to 28 days. We maintain 6 weeks of safety stock for core ingredients and 4 weeks for packaging.

## Pricing

Our wholesale pricing sheet is available as **wholesale-pricing.pdf** — click it in the sidebar to view it inline. We update it quarterly based on ingredient costs and MOQ changes.

## Fulfillment

- **Warehouse:** 3PL partner in Oakland, CA
- **Shipping:** USPS Priority (2–3 days) for subscription boxes, UPS Ground for wholesale
- **Packaging:** Compostable wrappers from PackRight Inc (see suppliers.csv)
- **Returns:** Full refund on first order, store credit after that

## People Ops

Current team scheduling and overtime expectations are documented in [[Working Hours and Overtime]].

## AI Agents

Our AI agents help monitor supplier updates, flag cost changes, and draft restock orders. Check the Agent Dashboard in the sidebar to see who's active and what they're working on.
