# Product

Premium candy bars, honest ingredients, direct to your door.

ChocoBar Co is a DTC candy brand that sells individual bars, variety packs, and build-your-own subscription boxes through our online store. We source single-origin cocoa and use real ingredients — no palm oil, no artificial flavors, no compromises.

## Product Principles

- **Taste first** — if it doesn't taste amazing, it doesn't ship
- **Radical transparency** — every ingredient sourced, every origin named
- **Accessible premium** — $4–6 price point, not $12 artisan pricing
- **Subscription-native** — recurring revenue through build-your-own boxes
